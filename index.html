Background

I have been making games since October, 2022. Over the past two years, I have been a part of 12 different game projects as an artist, leader, and technical artist. Through each of these projects, I learned how to work with others when making games so that I could focus most on the skills I needed to become a technical artist in the future. I've learned important skills including lighting, importing assets, shader creation, and rigging/animation. My goal for the future is to work within an environmental pipeline where I create tools and work closely with 3D artists as a technical artist.
