Nathan Gardea - Fledge

Spooky Blank Jam Oct, 2025
Collaborated with 4 others Itch.io Link

Project Summary - Play as Detective Crowe, a detective tasked with solving the murder of her own detective partner, who must gather information from witnesses and suspects to make a final decision on who the murderer might be. Interrogate, intimidate, and investigate your way through the bleak world of Fledge, and solve the mystery of who killed Crowe's partner.
